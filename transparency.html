1. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Instead of relying on general knowledge, LawBit retrieves specific, verified legal data from our vector database before generating an answer.

2. Privacy: FHE Layer

We perform mathematical predictions on encrypted data. The server processes the "dark" data and returns a result without ever decrypting or exposing your raw inputs.

3. Zero-Trust Verification

Every response is cryptographically signed. We ensure that the prediction hasn't been tampered with from the moment it leaves our secure compute environment.
